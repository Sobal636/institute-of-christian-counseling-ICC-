Welcome to ICC,


Our mission is to help you prioritize your mental health and find balance. We are dedicated to empowering you with the best answers to your most pressing health and wellness questions. For more than 10 years, we’ve worked hard to train 5 batches and trained more than 500 students from all over the India. We trained Doctors, nurses, Police officers, School teachers, Collage professors, Pastors, church leaders IT professional journalist etc.

The goal of ICC
The goal of The Institute of Christian Counseling is to return God’s Word to the centre of any setting where counselling, teaching, and discipleship occurs. We want to help the Church reclaim this all important spiritual territory, embracing it’s responsibility to restore the fallen (Gal 6:1-2) and evangelize the lost (Matt 28:19-20). Rather than refer hurting individuals and families to secular resources, we hope to see the Church return as the primary source of counselling and teaching for its community.
The Objective of ICC
Our objective is to train a growing number of candidates each year to rightly apply God’s Word in all counselling settings including the church, discipleship programs, youth and family ministries, the mission field and other Christian settings. The Biblical Counselling Institute seeks also to generate leaders and teachers within this field of ministry who may continue to advance the message of Biblical sufficiency throughout the INDIA and overseas.
